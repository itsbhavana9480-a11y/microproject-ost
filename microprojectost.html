Installation of notepad++

 1. Go to the official website.
  






 2. Open the downloaded .exe file and Click Yes if prompted (User Account Control).







 3. Select your preferred language → Click OK.







 4. Click Next







 5. Accept the license agreement → Click Next.







 6. Choose installation location (default is fine) → Click Next
.






 7. Select components (you can keep default settings).







 8. Click Install.







 9. After installation, click Finish.
